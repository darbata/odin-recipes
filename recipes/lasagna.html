Lasagna Recipe

Garfield likes eating lasagna. Let's make him some more!

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Sauce
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta sheets
  2. Cook beef in pan
  3. Add sauce and stir
  4. Mix egg and cheese
  5. Layer sauce, pasta and cheese
  6. Repeat for all layers
  7. Bake
  8. Let rest and enjoy!
